How it Works

1

You place your order


You choose your order type and area to see all the kitchens that operate in that area and their menus and place your order with only three total clicks.

2

Homechef starts cooking


Mumms’ homechefs receive your order instantly and start cooking you delicious meals, all prepared in their pre-approved kitchens. All the kitchens go through a serious process of hygiene and safety checks

3

Order delivery


One of Mumm’s delivery guys will pick up your order from the homechef’s kitchen and deliver it to your doorstep
